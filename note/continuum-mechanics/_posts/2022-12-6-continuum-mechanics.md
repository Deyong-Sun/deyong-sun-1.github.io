## 1. Kinematics
* Deformation gradient

$$ \mathbf{F}(\mathbf{X},t)=\Delta \chi = \frac{\partial}{\partial \mathbf{X}} \chi(\mathbf{X},t)  $$

* Determinant of $\mathnf{F}$
$$ J \equiv det(\frac{\partial \chi}{\partial \mathbf{X}}) =det \mathbf{F}\neq 0 $$
